Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule a consultation?
You can schedule a consultation by filling out the contact form above, calling us directly, or booking through our website. We'll get back to you within 24 hours to confirm your appointment.
What is your design process?
Our design process includes initial consultation, concept development, detailed planning, and construction oversight. We work closely with you at every stage to ensure your vision is realized.
How much do your services cost?
Our fees vary based on project scope and complexity. We offer transparent pricing and provide detailed quotes after the initial consultation. Contact us for a personalized estimate.
Do you work with clients outside Bangalore?
Yes, we work with clients across India. We conduct virtual meetings and make site visits as needed. Distance is not a barrier to creating your dream home.
How long does a typical project take?
Project timelines vary based on size and complexity. A typical home design project takes 3-6 months from concept to final plans. Construction timelines depend on various factors.
Do you offer renovation services?
Yes, we specialize in both new construction and renovations. Whether you want to remodel a single room or completely transform your home, we can help.
